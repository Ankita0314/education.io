Welcome To The Board of Open Schooling & Skill Education

The Board Of Open Scooling and Skills Education (BOSSE). Sikkim is an open schooling board that aim to cater to the varied academic needs of the divargent group of student up to pre-degree level including secondary/ seniour secondary,skills and vocatinal educational. BOSSE is open schooling board in sikkim. was established under act No.14 of 2020 of the sikkim legislative assembly. Passed on 21-09-2020, According to the sikkim act 2020 to promulgate and disseminate the open scholling education at state & National level
